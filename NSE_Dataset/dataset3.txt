The dataframe consists of the details of the activity of 10,000 traders.

There are 5 features in the dataframe and 2 response variables. 

The 5 features are:
1) avg_vol: average volume traded
2) avg_buy_vol: average buy volume
3) avg_value: average value traded
4) perc_ret: percentage return
5) avg_num_trades: average number of trades made

The two response variables are:
1) anomaly : Gives a 1 or 0 as an anomaly flag for the trader (1 = anomalous)
2) avg_num_orders: Gives the average number of orders placed by the trader
